About Me

I am an astrophysicist and Forrest Fellow at the International Centre for Radio Astronomy Research (ICRAR) at the University of Western Australia. I received my PhD at the University of Victoria in Canada. Before joining ICRAR, I was a Kavli Fellow at the Institute for the Physics and Mathematics of the Universe (IPMU) at the University of Tokyo.

My research focuses on applying AI to large volumes of data from top telescopes and supercomputer simulations to reveal how galaxies form and the physics of dark matter.

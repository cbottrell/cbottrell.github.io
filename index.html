About Me

I am an astrophysicist with expertise in galaxy dynamics, numerical simulations, and computational methods. My research focuses on understanding the formation and evolution of galaxies through advanced statistical techniques and high-performance computing.

I have a strong background in observational data analysis, cosmological simulations, and machine learning applications in astronomy. I'm passionate about using data-driven approaches to answer fundamental questions about our universe.

Research Interests

  • Galaxy morphology and structure
  • Dynamical modeling and simulations
  • Machine learning in astronomy
  • Large-scale structure and cosmology
  • Data-driven astrophysics
